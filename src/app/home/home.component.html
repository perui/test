Every day a person goes to a job they dislike or have no job to go to at all, is a loss. Not only for the individual, but also for the employer who has the potential to grow if they find the right person. This Sweden can not afford. 

We believe in using technology to let people connect in new ways. It will help people find their dream job and it will make employers find top candidates easier. By this our collective talents are better used and Sweden becomes a better place to live.

Guiding principles

No matter what we do, these principles will guide us:

  • Collaborate
    Work with others, they know something you do not know.
  • Diversity by design
    Everyone is unique; one-size solution does not fit all. 
  • Keep it simple
    If people understand, they can contribute!
